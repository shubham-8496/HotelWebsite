How To Reach

Bus
From Central bus stand/ Central bus depot and Thakkar Bazaar bus stand travel towards Mumbai Naka signal, travel straight to Panadvleni Caves i.e. towards Mumbai on the National Highway No 3, and go straight to reach Express Inn Nashik.
From Mahamarg bus stand, travel towards Panadvleni Caves i.e towards Mumbai on the National Highway No 3, and go straight to reach Express Inn Nashik

Trains
rom the Nashik Road railway station, travel towards Dwarka circle and reach Mumbai Naka signal. From the Mumbai Naka signal, get on to Mumbai Agra Highway road heading towards Panadavleni caves, Go straight to reach Express Inn Nashik.

Airport
From the airport, reach Mumbai-Agra highway and travel towards Dwarka circle. From Dwarka circle, move towards Mumbai Naka signal; get on to Mumbai Agra highway road and go straight heading towards Panadavleni caves to reach Express Inn Nashik.

Distance
6.5 km from the Central bus stand, Central bus depot and Thakkar Bazaar bus stand.
5.4 km from the Mahamarg bus stand.
12.3 km from the Nashik Road railway station.
